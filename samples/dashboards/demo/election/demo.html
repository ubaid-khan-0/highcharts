Election demo

The demo shows the results from US presidential elections.

How to use

States are selected by clicking on the map. The election year is selected by the dropdown.

Dashboards components

  • Custom HTML and Chart for the national results or state results (with pictures and dual-colored bar)
  • Custom HTML provides a dropdown for the election year and a checkbox to select all states.
  • Highcharts Maps shows the winning party per state, color-coded Democrat and Republican.
  • Highcharts Chart shows historical national or state results.
  • Dashboards Datagrid shows detailed results of all states. In the grid the electoral vote may be changed.
